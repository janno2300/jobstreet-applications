Generate and manage 3rd-party app password
(https://help.yahoo.com/kb/SLN15241.html)

To get a Yahoo app password, you can: 

1. Sign in to your Yahoo Account Security page. (https://login.yahoo.com/myaccount/security/)
2. Click Generate app password or Generate and manage app passwords.
3. Click Get Started.
4. Enter your app's name in the text field.
5. Click Generate password.
6. Use the one-time password to log in to your 3rd party app .
7. Click Done.
